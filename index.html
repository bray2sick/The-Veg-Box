How The Veg Box Works

Simple steps to get fresh produce delivered to your door

1

Browse & Select

Explore fresh produce from local farmers in your area. Choose from vegetables, fruits, herbs, and artisanal products.

2

Order & Pay

Add items to your veg box and checkout securely. Support local producers with every purchase.

3

Receive Fresh

Get your fresh produce delivered 3 times a week using our eco-friendly electric vehicles.
